# Customer Journey Optimization: Executive Summary

## Situation

A European retail bank acquires customers through 6 channels and guides them through a 6-step digital onboarding funnel. Over 8,000 prospects were analyzed across a 12-month period.

## Complication

80% of prospects drop off before completing onboarding. The KYC document upload step alone loses 2,300 prospects. Affiliate and Paid Social channels cost 9x more per conversion than Referral, with no evidence of higher customer quality.

## Key Findings

**1. Referral Program Delivers the Best ROI**
Referral converts at 30% with a cost of EUR 83 per conversion, outperforming every other channel on both rate and efficiency.

**2. Affiliate and Paid Social Are Burning Budget**
Affiliates cost EUR 733 per conversion at 8.2% conversion. Paid Social costs EUR 755 at 11.3%. Together they consume disproportionate budget relative to the customers they deliver.

**3. KYC Is a Design Problem, Not a Customer Problem**
"Form too long" is the #1 dropout reason (1,384 people). A simulated A/B test of a simplified KYC form showed a statistically significant 5.4% conversion lift (p = 0.028).

## Recommendations

| Action | Expected Impact | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Double referral program investment | +200 conversions per year at EUR 83 each | **High** |
| Exit bottom 50% of affiliate partners | Save EUR 80K+ annually | **High** |
| Mobile-first KYC redesign with camera scan | +8% mobile step conversion | **Medium** |
